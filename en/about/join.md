# SiiWay Recruitment

## Background
Currently (Jan 22, 2025), SiiWay Team has only three core members. During winter break, xhc861 proposed a recruitment plan, unanimously approved by all team members. Thus, wyf9 and xhc861 co-authored this announcement to recruit new members.

## Promotion
We will publish this announcement on the following platforms:

- Official website
- Discord
- QQ Group
- ...

## Why join us?
Join our team to discuss learning topics, explore the internet, and develop open-source projects together.

In short, you'll find a group of like-minded peers online -- doesn't that sound appealing?

> Not only that, we also host occasional community events that everyone can join!

## Requirements
We have the following hard requirements for team members:

- Be friendly
- Willing to accept others' suggestions
- Don't blindly follow trends
- *Don't poke into things you shouldn't*
- Comply with the **[SiiWay Code of Conduct](./code-of-conduct)**

Of course, meeting the following is ideal:

- Solid computer science fundamentals
- Programming skills (e.g. `Python`, `TypeScript`, `C#`, `Rust`, ...)
- GitHub account (and other social platforms)

## Disclaimer
Before joining, please be aware of the following:

1. *Core members are minors and may not always be online*
2. This is a pure tech interest group, *not a formal organization*
3. **We are non-profit and will not provide any monetary compensation or material rewards to team members**
4. **If individual actions cause irreversible damage to the team, we reserve the right to hold them accountable**
5. **We do not restrict members' political views, religious beliefs (or sexual orientation), but publishing *any* content under the team's name that damages our reputation is strictly prohibited. Once discovered, severe penalties will apply.**
6. We will securely handle your information.
