### `QQ_CHANNEL_BANNED`

The QQ Channel (Tencent Channel) has been banned.

### `DISCORD_SERVER_BANNED`

The Discord server has been banned.

### `TELEGRAM_GROUP_BANNED`

The Telegram group has been banned.

### `TELEGRAM_CHANNEL_BANNED`

The Telegram channel has been banned.
